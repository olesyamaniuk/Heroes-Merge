FAQ

  • Is Heroes Merge free to play?
    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes! You can enjoy the game even without an internet connection.
  • How do I unlock new heroes?
    Earn rewards by winning battles, completing challenges, and progressing in the game.
  • Are there multiplayer battles?
    You can compete against other players and climb the leaderboards.
  • Will there be new updates?
    Absolutely! We regularly add new heroes, features, and challenges.
  • What happens if I lose a battle?
    Losing a battle won’t reset your progress. You can refine your strategy, upgrade your heroes, and try again!
  • Are there different types of heroes?
    Yes! Each hero has unique abilities and strengths, allowing you to create the perfect battle strategy.
  • Can I customize my heroes?
    While you can’t fully customize their appearance, you can upgrade their abilities and unlock new versions through merging.
  • How do leaderboards work?
    The more battles you win, the higher your ranking. Compete in events to earn bonus points and climb the global leaderboard.
  • Is there a limit to how many heroes I can merge?
    No! You can continuously merge and upgrade heroes to build the strongest army possible.
